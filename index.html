Check Out This Interactive Movie Map!

For the best visual effects for the world map, we recommend you use a mouse to zoom in and hover.

Hover over a country to see the total number of movies in our data that were produced from that specific country, as well as the number of movies produced in that country that were streamed on Netflix, Hulu, Amazon Prime, and Disney Plus. To see a more regionally detailed view of the map zoom, in and out with the mouse. Through this visualization, an analysis of the distribution of the movies accross the world.

Check Out These Top Movies From 2000-2021!

Select a year from the dropdown menu below to see the top 10 Shows or Movies for the given year, according to the IMDb Rating. The peach bar indicates the highest rated movie from that specific year. Some of the years may have multiple peach bars highighted due to many movies with the same highest rating. Hover over the interactive bars to see the movie title and the rating of the movie. The interactive line can be used to compare movie ratings.
